Bitewing, Anterior, Posterior

Conversation

Before the dentist enters:

  • Ask patient main complaint:
    • What type of pain
      • Pain when biting or chewing
      • Sensitivity to cold or hot
    • When did the pain start, and how long it has been present
      • Check if any recent treatment was done and whether discomfort started before or after that visit
      • Ask when was their last dental checkup
  • If patient not sure of the exact tooth
    • Perform precaution test
  • Take PA of the area prior to calling dentist
