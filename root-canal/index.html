Conversation

Before the dentist enters:

  • Review changes in medical history, allergies, and medications
  • Confirm tooth number and procedure
  • Make sure there is a PA of the tooth prior to beginning of treatment
  • Walk patient through what to expect
    • Longer appointment ~1.5 hours
    • If the treatment becomes more complex or there is a severe infection, the patient may need to return to complete the procedure
    • Basic explanation of RCT:
      • "RCT is a filling for your nerve. When a cavity has compromised the nerve but the tooth structure is still savable, we can clean out the infected nerve canal, place some filling material inside, and then fill the tooth back up."

After treatment:

  • Make sure there is a final PA of the tooth
  • Confirm with doctor whether tooth requires a crown
