Conversation

Before the dentist enters:

  • Review changes in medical history, allergies, and medications
  • Confirm procedure and tooth number
  • Take initial impression via heavy body
  • Ask about pain or concerns they want to address
  • Inform patient it takes two weeks for crown to be made
  • Temporary crown instruction to inform patient
    • Appearance: temporary crown will look less natural but necessary for protection
    • Hygiene: no flossing or brushing tooth if possible
    • If the temporary comes off early: return to office immediately
      • Patient must come back if there is significant time before their insertion appointment
    • If temporary comes off 2-3 days before insertion: okay to wait
      • Patient must avoid biting or eating hard/crunchy foods on that side until insertion appointment
