Conversation

Before the dentist enters:

  • Confirm procedure and tooth number
  • Ask about sensitivity or discomfort since last visit
  • Depends on the doctor
    • Remove temporary crown
      • Clean the area and remove residual temporary cement
    • Take X-ray with new crown
      • REMOVE the crown immediately after the X-ray to prevent accidental swallowing

After treatment:

  • Things to let the patient know about their crown
    • No eating or drinking for 1 hour
    • Cementation takes 24 hours to fully set
      • In the meantine, avoid flossing and chewing on that side
    • For enterior crowns (espcially 11 and 21)
      • The crowns are for cosmetic purposes only
      • Completely avoid chewing or biting with their front teeth
